A pleasure to meet you!

I'm Mikee Nguyen, an aspiring programmer and scientist at 25 years old.

Some of my hobbies include snowboarding, reading, video games, acting, and short-film production.

I graduated from UCSD with a B.S. in General Biology and a minor in business. I've worked for various research and biotech organizations including Salk Institute, SG Biofuels, Center for Aquatic Technologies, and BTS Research.

My time at UCSD was well-spent; I served as Co-president for the Biological Sciences Student Association (BSSA), as well as President for Phi Sigma Pi National Honor Fraternity. I was also a transfer orientation leader (Eleanor Roosevelt College) and a teacher aide for both lower and upper div evolutionary biology.

I am currently employed as a software engineer intern for TargetSolutions, an e-learning based company that provides training and management resources for fire departments, police departments, EMS professionals, security firms, contractors, educational staff, and other similar fields in which safety and service to the public are top priority!
