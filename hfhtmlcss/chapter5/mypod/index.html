Welcome to Zeta Alpha

The nation's only Honors Co-ed Fraternity, Phi Sigma Pi has a glowing presence here in the West Coast at UCSD's very own Chapter, Zeta Alpha. A member of the Pacific Region, Zeta Alpha Chapter was the first to be awarded the Top in Region, competing against 11 peer Chapter members. Be sure to visit our website for more information about who we are and how to join.
